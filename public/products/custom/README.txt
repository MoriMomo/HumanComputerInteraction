Drop your future product images in this folder.

Recommended format:
- .webp (best for size and quality)
- .avif (smaller, modern)
- .jpg/.png (fallback)

Suggested naming:
- cardholder-pro.webp
- wallet-elite.webp
- desk-organizer.webp

Public URL pattern in app code:
/products/custom/<filename>

Example:
/products/custom/cardholder-pro.webp
